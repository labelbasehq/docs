### Activate the app

First of all, go to your integrations on LabelBase, choose a label, and activate the Discord app.

![Capture D’écran 2025 12 31 À 09 38 36](/images/Captured’écran2025-12-31à09.38.36.png)

### Follow the instructions

![Capture D’écran 2025 12 31 À 09 38 48](/images/Captured’écran2025-12-31à09.38.48.png)

![Capture D’écran 2025 12 31 À 09 39 08](/images/Captured’écran2025-12-31à09.39.08.png)

This will first, install the bot on your server, and connect it to your label.

### How to send a submission to LabelBase ?

When you receive a submission, in any channel, just right click on the submission (link, message etc...), choose "App" \> "Send to LabelBase"

![Capture D’écran 2025 12 31 À 09 39 24](/images/Captured’écran2025-12-31à09.39.24.png)

### Prompt the artist

LabelBase Bot will automatically prompt the artist to collect mandatory infos for the submission.

![Capture D’écran 2025 12 31 À 09 39 35](/images/Captured’écran2025-12-31à09.39.35.png)

### Confirmation

When received, the bot will send a confirmation message.

![Capture D’écran 2025 12 31 À 09 39 44](/images/Captured’écran2025-12-31à09.39.44.png)

The submission will be available in LabelBase. You can manage it.

### Ticketing tool

> If you are using a ticketing tool for your submission, you'll need to grant access to created channel to the LabelBase bot. So the bot will be automatically added to any created channel by the ticketing tool.
