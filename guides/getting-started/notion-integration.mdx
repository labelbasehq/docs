### First : create a notion integration

Go on https://www.notion.so/profile/integrations

And click "New Integration"

![Capture D’écran 2026 01 06 À 15 42 10](/images/Captured’écran2026-01-06à15.42.10.png)

### Then : Setup your integration

![Capture D’écran 2026 01 06 À 15 42 50](/images/Captured’écran2026-01-06à15.42.50.png)

Use type internal and select your workspace.\
Adding a picture is not mandatory.

### Get your secret key

![Capture D’écran 2026 01 06 À 15 43 45](/images/Captured’écran2026-01-06à15.43.45.png)

Get your secret key and copy it.

<Info>
  We store it in our DB, but it will be encrypted. No one will be able to read it, including us.
</Info>

### Activate the notion integration

Go on your integrations on labelbase, select the label you want, and activate the integration

![Capture D’écran 2026 01 06 À 15 45 00](/images/Captured’écran2026-01-06à15.45.00.png)

![Capture D’écran 2026 01 06 À 15 45 51](/images/Captured’écran2026-01-06à15.45.51.png)

Then paste the secret key. Then you will have all explanation on how to sync your notion database to labelbase.
